Eating


If you plan to go to bed by 11:00, stop eating at 8:00. Plan to not have three hours between your last meal of the day and the time you plan to sleep.
Having a smaller dinner and spreading out those calories throughout the day may also help prevent weight gain from late meals.

Music


Lullabies and soft tunes used on children can be translated to adult lives to aid in having sounder sleep

In one study, adults who listened to 45 minutes of music before going to sleep reported having better sleep quality beginning on the very first night.

Even more encouraging is that this benefit appears to have a cumulative effect with study participants reporting better sleep the more often they incorporated music into their nightly routine.

Decrease in time it takes to fall asleep: 27 to 69 minutes to fall asleep versus 6 to 13 minutes with music.

Why does this work? The ability to hear music depends on a series of steps that convert sound waves coming into the ear into electrical signals in the brain.
Music triggers the release of dopamine and regulates the hormones in a person.
